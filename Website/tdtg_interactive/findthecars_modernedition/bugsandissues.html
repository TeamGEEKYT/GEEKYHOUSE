Known Issues and bugs

This page lists all known bugs and issues from Find The Cars: Modern Edition either reported by our players in our Discord Server, either discovered by ourselves.


UI cannot be hidden at the same time as the music is muted

This is a weird bug where you can only mute the game music or hide the UI, but you can't do both.


Blur while no UI is shown

Issue has been found and will be patched on a future update.


[FIXED] Being for too long in a server might make you lag, and eventually crash

The issue has been fixed.


Upon entering in ??? Map, the Shop Button is not hidden

Issue has been found and will be patched on a future update.


Money UI can be visible upon entering the ??? Map

If you hide and show again the UI, this UI will be visible. Issue has been found and will be patched on a future update.


Grab&Go music while outside of it

If you stick on a Grab&Go wall, the music might randomly stop and trigger the In-shop-music. Issue has been found and will be patched on a future update.


[FIXED] Grab&Go music triggers for everyone

The issue has been solved.


[FIXED] Kill parts from secret obby not having hitboxes

The issue has been solved.

[FIXED] Dying from a kill part allows you to collect cars

If you die, for example at the last Kill Part of the Hard Obby, and you manage to touch a car right before respawning, you'll still get the car.

The issue has been solved.

Donation button does not work anymore


Invisible Wall in Impossible Secret Obby

Issue has been found and will be solved on a future update.
